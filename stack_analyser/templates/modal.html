About & Help

Hello, welcome to my Stackoverflow Analyser !

It's a simple web based project writen in python, bootstrap and jQuery. I use scrapy to crawl nearly 800 million questions from the famous Q&A site stackoverflow. Then I spend a lot of time to find out what on earth the site has and the result is what you have seen.

Now with Stackoverflow Analyser, it's easy for you to learn:

  • What's the most frequently asked topics?
  • How much share every topic account for?
  • How the attention of every topic change with time?

If you want to know how I make it, you may download the ppt for more information. And I have put the source code on GitHub, any communication is welcome!
